Then, there exist indices $i \in I$ and $t \in \{0,\ldots,|T|-s_i\} $ with $x_i^t > \displaystyle 0.5\,\left( \sum_{h=1}^{s_i} x_i^{t+h} +1\right)$.

\]
as $h' \in [\max \{1, t+h'-(|T|-s_i)\}, \min\{s_i,t+h'\}]$.
